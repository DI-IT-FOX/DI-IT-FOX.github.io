Thinking about the life.


Our life is the feelings and emotions,

series of various events;

All in a lifetime

And we always should rejoice at life.


If the path to the goal is hard

We need to try to achieve this goal;

Everything necessarily will turn

But we should never give up too.


If you follow your dream

And your conscience is clear

You can safely say

That you don't live in vain!


15th of August 2015.
